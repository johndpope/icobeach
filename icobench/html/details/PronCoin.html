About PronCoin

We're solving many problems! Beside decentralizing the whole system, we're avoiding bank fees, high credit card fees, chargebacks that are the biggest problem in adult online industry. To pay each user for contributing to adult community, with current online adult system would be very hard. Each user should make a contract with company, everyone have different payment options, some of them are not accepting adult transactions, like PayPal for example. So by paying you in Pron tokens and sending money to your wallet is easy, fast, almost no fees and no contract needed. All payments will be handled by smart contracts and will be deployed automatically. How you will spend that money and where is up to you! And the biggest problem we're solving is, that any user of platforms will be able to earn PronCoins and will be able to earn money. Until now, this was not the business model in online adult industry, because of complicated payment structure to each individual. And the last problem we're solving is protecting any user of adult platform, that there wont be any hidden charges as most of the sites are doing now. So usually when you signup to one site membership, you also get charged and get access to some other sites. But you didn't know, because there is a small print and you didn't pay attention on the join page. So we're solving this with smartcontracts and there wont be any hidden charges with our payment system.
